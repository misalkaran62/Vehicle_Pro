FAQ's

Login and Account Management

1. I'm having trouble logging into my TMS account. What should I do?

Ensure you're using the correct username and password. If you've forgotten your credentials, use the "Forget Password" link to reset your password. If issues persist, contact support for assistance.

2. How do I reset my password?

Click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page, follow the instructions sent to your registered email, and create a new password.

3. Can I change my username or email address?

Yes, you can usually update your username or email address through your account settings or by contacting support for assistance.
